Xiaomin Wang

I am a PhD student majoring in applied mathematics at Beijing Normal University.

My research focuses on cooperation in asymmetric games. I study how heterogeneity in different dimensions of individuals affects cooperation through game theory and behavioral experiments.

You can contact me at wangxiaomin@mail.bnu.edu.cn .
